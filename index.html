<About/>

My name is Leonnardo Nascimento, I'm a full stack developer with a background in graphic design. Since I was 14 years old, I had the desire to work with technology. Since then, playing video games on the computer has been one of my favorite hobbies, but my creativity has always been present. My first experience was making drawings and thumbnails for YouTube creators, then I started editing their videos. But it wasn't until I moved to the United States of America that I could start paying my Graphic Designer bills in a small store in my town. But I felt I should learn and understand more about the impact of software in general, that's when I decided to participate in a bootcamp at Coding Dojo.
